# 脱敏说明 / Anonymization Notice

## 中文

本仓库面向公开分享，已按以下原则处理敏感信息：

- 不提交原始 PDF、原始厂商说明书、合同、截图、生产配置或密钥。
- 不包含真实患者数据、医院内部网络信息、账号、接口凭据或日志。
- 第三方厂商名称统一替换为“国内厂商 A/B/C/D”或“国际厂商 A/B/C/D/E/F”。
- 删除或替换可能反推出具体第三方厂商的官网链接。
- 保留的是能力分类、工程方法、需求结构、风险管理和交付体系。

标准、法规和开放互操作组织名称，例如 HL7、DICOM、IHE、IEC、ISO，属于公开标准或组织引用，未按厂商名称处理。

## English

This repository is prepared for public sharing and follows these anonymization rules:

- Original PDFs, vendor manuals, contracts, screenshots, production configuration, and secrets are not committed.
- No real patient data, hospital network details, accounts, interface credentials, or logs are included.
- Third-party vendor names are replaced with generic labels such as “Domestic Vendor A/B/C/D” or “International Vendor A/B/C/D/E/F”.
- Links that could identify specific third-party vendors are removed or generalized.
- The repository preserves capability categories, engineering methods, requirement structure, risk management, and delivery governance.

Names of public standards or interoperability organizations, such as HL7, DICOM, IHE, IEC, and ISO, are retained because they refer to standards bodies rather than third-party product vendors.
