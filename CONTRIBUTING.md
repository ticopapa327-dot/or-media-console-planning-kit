# 贡献说明 / Contributing

## 中文

欢迎围绕软件工程方法、需求拆解、架构规划、测试验证、风险管理和 Codex 工作流提出改进。

提交内容时请遵守：

- 不提交真实患者数据、医院内部资料、生产配置、密钥或未授权资料。
- 不恢复第三方厂商名称、商标、官网链接或可识别产品名称。
- 新增需求、风险、测试和上线项时使用稳定编号。
- 涉及医疗、安全、隐私或合规的内容请注明假设和适用边界。
- 复杂变更请同时更新追踪矩阵和相关文档。

## English

Contributions are welcome for software-engineering practices, requirements decomposition, architecture planning, testing, risk management, and Codex workflows.

Please follow these rules:

- Do not commit real patient data, internal hospital materials, production configuration, secrets, or unauthorized materials.
- Do not restore third-party vendor names, trademarks, official links, or identifiable product names.
- Use stable identifiers when adding requirements, risks, tests, or release-gate items.
- Clearly state assumptions and boundaries for healthcare, security, privacy, or compliance content.
- For complex changes, update the traceability matrix and related documents.
